The Band

Welcome to the City of Hong Kong Pipe Band! Since we started in 2013, our passion for Scottish bagpipe music has been the heartbeat of our ensemble. We're all about sharing the joy and tradition of the pipes, whether we're lighting up the stage, joining in a community celebration, or adding a special touch to private gatherings. Dive into the melody and spirit of our band, and see how we can make your next event unforgettable.

Service Offerings

Elevate your occasion with the City of Hong Kong Pipe Band's array of services, each designed to add a special note to your event:
  • Weddings: Enhance your special day with the romance of the bagpipes, creating memories that last a lifetime.
  • Galas: Add a touch of sophistication to your evening events with our elegant pipe tunes.
  • Anniversaries: Celebrate milestones with the timeless sound of our music, marking each year with tradition.
  • Festivals: Bring joy and festivity to your celebrations with our upbeat and lively performances.
  • Business Promotions: Make your brand stand out with the unique backdrop of our bagpipe melodies.
  • Events: From formal gatherings to casual meetups, our music sets the perfect tone for any event.
  • Welcoming Guests: Greet your guests with the dignified and warm sound of bagpipes, making every entrance grand.

For those seeking a tailored experience or specific event format, we are more than happy to accommodate custom requests. Our team is prepared to work closely with you to ensure your event's musical needs are met with precision and grace. Please contact us to discuss how we can customize our services to create an unforgettable atmosphere for your event.

Explore Our Service Plans

Let the City of Hong Kong Pipe Band create the perfect soundtrack for your event. Choose from our range of service plans to match the scale and atmosphere you desire. Each plan is accompanied by a visual glimpse of what we offer, allowing you to envision the ambiance we can provide.

Service Demonstration

Solo Piper

Solo Piper A lone piper stands, the echo of solitude in every note. A single melody weaves through the air, pure and unaccompanied, its haunting beauty resonating with the simplicity of a singular performance.

Duo of Pipers

Double the melody, double the impact. Two pipers in harmony provide a fuller sound and a stirring presence.

Quartet of Pipers

Experience the rich layers of four pipers as they weave intricate musical patterns that captivate and enchant.

Mini Band

Our mini band option includes four pipers complemented by the resonant beats of two drummers. The perfect balance of melody and rhythm.

Full Band

For a truly grand affair, the full band presents an impressive ensemble of pipers and drummers, tailored to the size of your event for maximum impact.

For more details on each plan and to discuss the specific requirements for your event, please contact us. We're excited to work with you to craft a memorable musical experience that perfectly suits your celebration.
